Needle is a utility that builds copyable search queries for Google, YouTube, and Reddit. By using Needle you agree to these Terms of Use.

What Needle provides

Needle generates search text based on the choices you enter. You can copy that text and paste it into the search service of your choice, or tap Open to launch a search URL in Safari or another app. Needle does not scrape results, guarantee rankings, or control how Google, YouTube, or Reddit interpret a query.

Needle Pro subscription

Needle includes a limited number of free search builds. Needle Pro unlocks unlimited search building as an auto-renewable monthly subscription. Payment is charged to your Apple Account at confirmation of purchase. The subscription renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage or cancel in Settings under Subscriptions. Any unused portion of a free trial, if offered, is forfeited when you purchase a subscription.

Restore Purchases

If you previously subscribed, use Restore Purchases to restore an active Needle Pro entitlement on this device.

Acceptable use

Use Needle only for lawful purposes. Do not misuse the app to violate the terms of third-party search platforms.

Disclaimer

Needle is provided as is. Search platforms may rank, rewrite, or broaden results. Needle does not promise perfect or complete search results.
