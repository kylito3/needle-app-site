Help for Needle Search Builder

Needle builds search queries for Google, YouTube, and Reddit that you can copy or open. It does not search the web for you, use AI, or store search history.

Common questions

  • How do I use a search? Complete the guided flow, tap Build My Search, then tap Copy Search to paste—or Open in Google, YouTube, or Reddit.
  • What is Needle Pro? A monthly subscription that unlocks unlimited search building after the free limit.
  • How do I restore a purchase? Open Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases.
  • How do I cancel? On your iPhone, go to Settings, tap your name, then Subscriptions, then Needle Pro.
